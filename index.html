Deloitte Data Analysis: Telemetry & Pay Equality

Problem:

How can Daikibo better understand machine failure patterns and address internal concerns about gender pay inequality?

Process:
  • Analyzed one month of telemetry JSON data from factories in Japan, Germany and China.
  • Identified breakdown trends by location and machine type.
  • Used Excel to classify gender equality scores across job roles.
  • Built a Tableau dashboard to visualize failures and Excel to show fairness levels.
Result:

Revealed the most failure-prone factory and job roles with critical pay disparities. Findings informed both operational and HR strategy.
